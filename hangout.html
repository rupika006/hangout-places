Charminar

Charminar Rd, Char Kaman, Ghansi Bazaar, Hyderabad
🕐 Daily: 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM

Built in 1591 by Sultan Mohammed Quli Qutb Shah, Charminar is Hyderabad's most famous landmark. While its exact purpose is debated, popular beliefs say it marked the end of a deadly plague, served as a tribute to Allah, commemorated the second Islamic millennium, and celebrated the foundation of Hyderabad.

The monument's four minarets (48.7 m high) represent the first four caliphs of Islam. Designed by Iranian architect Mir Momin Astrawadi, it's a square granite and lime structure with intricate carvings, arches, and a mosque on the top floor — the oldest in the city. Visitors can climb 149 steps for stunning city views.

✦ Legend speaks of a secret tunnel to Golconda Fort, though none has been found.

🏰

Golconda Fort

Khair Complex, Ibrahim Bagh, Hyderabad
🕐 Daily: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Originally built as a mud fort in 1143 by the Kakatiya rulers and called Mankal, Golconda was later expanded by the Bahmani Sultans and transformed into a massive granite fortress by the Qutub Shahi dynasty, who made it their capital. Once a thriving diamond-trading hub, it was famed for producing legendary gems like the Koh-i-Noor.

The fort spans 5 km in circumference, with 8 gateways, massive ramparts, royal palaces, mosques, and a hilltop pavilion 130 m high offering panoramic views. Its acoustics are an engineering marvel — a clap at the Fateh Darwaza can be heard 1 km away at the top pavilion.

✦ In 1687, Golconda fell to Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb, who left it in ruins.

🌊

Hussain Sagar

Hussain Sagar, Hyderabad, Telangana
🕐 Daily: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM

Also known as Tank Bund, Hussain Sagar is Asia's largest artificial lake, built in 1562 by Ibrahim Quli Qutb Shah and named after Hussain Shah Wali. Originally constructed for irrigation, it now connects Hyderabad and Secunderabad while serving as a beloved leisure spot.

At its center stands a striking 16 m tall, 350-ton white granite Buddha statue on the Rock of Gibraltar, beautifully illuminated at night. The lake is surrounded by parks like Lumbini Park, Sanjeevaiah Park, and Indira Park, making it perfect for evening strolls.

✦ The Buddha statue is one of the world's tallest monolithic statues.
